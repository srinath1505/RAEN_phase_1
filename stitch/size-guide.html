Size Guide

RAEN garments are designed with a considered relationship between structure and the body. Consult the measurements below, or write to us at hello@raen.design for a personal fit consultation.

Measurements

Size Bust (cm) Waist (cm) Hip (cm)
XS 78-82 60-64 86-90
S 82-86 64-68 90-94
M 86-90 68-72 94-98
L 90-94 72-76 98-102
XL 94-98 76-80 102-106

How to Measure

Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your bust.

Waist: Measure around your natural waistline.

Hip: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.

Personal Fit Consultation

If you are between sizes or would like guidance before purchasing, our atelier team is happy to assist. Write to us at hello@raen.design.
