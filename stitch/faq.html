Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the questions our clients ask most often. For anything not covered here, our atelier team is available at hello@raen.design.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of receipt. Pieces must be unworn and in original condition with all tags attached. Return shipping is complimentary worldwide.

Where do you ship?

We ship to over 200 countries. RAEN pieces are delivered to clients in Dubai, Monaco, London, Milan, Paris, Singapore, New York, and beyond.

How do I care for my silk garment?

RAEN silks require specialist dry cleaning. Store each piece in the provided dust bag, away from direct light. Do not machine wash. For full instructions, see our Garment Care page.

How is RAEN made?

Each piece is constructed by skilled artisans working with materials sourced from heritage mills. We hold our production partners to standards of craftsmanship we do not compromise.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and bank transfer. Payment is processed securely at checkout.

Do you offer bespoke commissions?

For private commissions and atelier appointments, please write to us at hello@raen.design. We will be in touch within 48 hours.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation with real-time tracking. Your piece is monitored from atelier to door.

Something we have not answered here? Our atelier team is always available.
